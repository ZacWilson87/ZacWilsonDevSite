About

Zac Wilson

Senior Software Engineer

"I'd rather ship something useful than architect something perfect."

Currently at Meta, building large-scale internal tools that serve thousands of researchers and admins. Five years in, I'm still most excited by the moment when messy requirements become working software.

My path wasn't linear. Design school dropout turned bootcamp graduate. Started by initially favoring frontend work, moved into full-stack roles, eventually found my footing in backend and infrastructure. Each step taught me something about what actually matters: reliability over cleverness, clarity over abstraction, momentum over perfection.

These days I work primarily in Go, React, and TypeScript. I've built IoT platforms for energy monitoring, agricultural data systems, and more internal dashboards than I can count. The common thread is always the same: understand the problem deeply, then build the simplest thing that solves it.

Based in Fayetteville, AR. United States
Experience 5+ years
Focus Full-Stack, Tooling, AI Integrations
