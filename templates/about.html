About

Zac Wilson

Senior Software Engineer

"I'd rather ship something useful than architect something perfect."

I build full-stack systems, robust APIs, and tooling. 5 years in, I'm still most excited by the moment when messy requirements become working software.

My path wasn't linear. Bootcamp graduate. Started by initially favoring frontend work (I blame my design background), moved into full-stack roles, eventually found my footing in backend and infrastructure. Each step taught me something about what actually matters: reliability over cleverness, clarity over abstraction, momentum over perfection.

These days I work primarily in Go, Python, and TypeScript. I've built payment systems, real-time data pipelines, CLI tools, and more internal dashboards than I can count. The common thread is always the same: understand the problem deeply, then build the simplest thing that solves it.
